Calalloo

Authenticity as a Protocol.

Calalloo is a Heritage Restoration Lab. We bridge the gap between deep Caribbean culinary traditions and technical precision, ensuring that the soul of our food is never lost to time or generic dilution.

The Heritage

We don't just share recipes; we restore protocols. From the specific "swizzle" of a Callaloo to the caramelization of sugar in a Pelau, we document the exact traditional methods passed down through generations.

The Sourcing

Provenance is everything. We map our ingredients back to their primary nodes—the farmers at Macoya, the Northern Wholesale markets, and the roadside vendors who extract coconut milk by hand. Authenticity starts in the soil.

The Lab

By treating heritage food as a high-performance provision, we optimize the bioavailability of traditional ingredients while honoring the culture that created them.
