The Science of Shadows: Mapping Culantro's DNA

Shado Beni, or Culantro, is the backbone of the Caribbean flavor profile. But beneath its serrated leaves lies a complex genetic history that varies from the hills of Paramin to the valleys of Guyana.

At Calalloo, our Neural Scanner doesn't just "see" a leaf; it identifies the regional variant. By mapping these culinary nodes, we are creating the first digital archive of Caribbean botanical heritage.

This data allows us to predict crop yields and ensure that local farmers are compensated fairly for the intelligence they have preserved for generations.
