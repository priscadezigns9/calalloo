Logistics of Flavor: The Breadfruit Supply Chain

Breadfruit is a Caribbean superfood, yet much of it goes to waste due to inefficient logistics. Calalloo's logistics engine identifies high-density crop areas and matches them with retail demand in real-time.

By connecting the scanner to local markets, we reduce food waste by 40% and ensure fresh provisions are available to those who need them most.
