Trinidadian Callaloo.

The crown jewel of Caribbean cuisine. A velvety, nutrient-dense blend of dasheen bush, okra, and coconut milk. This is performance nutrition in its most traditional form.

Verified Sourcing Nodes

Dasheen Bush: NAMDEVCO Macoya
Okra: Northern Wholesale Market
Coconut Milk: Roadside Artisanal Extraction

Ingredients

  • 1 Bundle Dasheen Bush (Chopped)Primary Source
  • 12 Fresh Okras (Sliced)Local Node
  • 1/4 lb Pumpkin (Cubed)High-Fiber
  • 2 cups Fresh Coconut MilkActive Fat
  • 1 Onion & 4 Cloves GarlicAromatics
  • 3 Pimento PeppersZest
  • 1 piece Smoked Bone or Salt MeatFlavor Vector
  • Salt & Black PepperRefiners

Lab Process

  1. Place dasheen bush, okra, pumpkin, coconut milk, and aromatics into the pressure vessel (or heavy pot).
  2. Add the flavor vector (smoked bone/meat) and submerge in the coconut base.
  3. Apply high thermal energy. If using a pressure cooker, seal and process for 15 minutes. If open pot, simmer for 45 minutes until cellular breakdown is complete.
  4. Once soft, use a "swizzle stick" or immersion blender to homogenize the mixture until velvety smooth.
  5. Adjust salinity and serve alongside performance rice or coo-coo.
